Bullet Classic

The Bullet Classic offers a perfect blend of traditional styling with modern performance. It features a powerful 346cc engine, retro looks, and a comfortable ride.

Bullet Standard

The Bullet Standard is an iconic model with a timeless design. Known for its reliability and rugged performance, it has been a favorite for decades.

Bullet Electra

The Bullet Electra combines classic aesthetics with modern engineering. It offers a smoother ride with its advanced suspension and refined engine.

Bullet Trials

The Bullet Trials is designed for those who love adventure. It features off-road capabilities with enhanced grip, stability, and handling for rugged terrains.
